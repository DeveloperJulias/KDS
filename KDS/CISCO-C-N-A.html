CISCO Certified Networking Associate

Kent's East African Digital Solution offers a comprehensive program to prepare students for the Cisco Certified Networking Associate (CCNA) certification. This program equips students with the skills and knowledge necessary to install, operate, and troubleshoot medium-sized routed and switched networks. The curriculum covers a wide range of networking topics, including network fundamentals, LAN switching technologies, routing technologies, infrastructure services, and infrastructure maintenance. Students will have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in the school's state-of-the-art networking labs, preparing them for the demands of the industry. With a focus on practical skills and real-world applications, Kent's East African Digital Solution is dedicated to ensuring that students are well-prepared to pursue a career in network administration and support.

Kents East African Digital Solutions provides computer services, courses, digital innovation, software development, IT consulting, and maintenance.
